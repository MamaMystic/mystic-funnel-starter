Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the Roadmap to Healing Strategy Call for?

This call is designed for burned-out caregivers, helpers, moms, nomads, and seekers who feel like they've lost their spark and are ready to invest in their own well-being. If you're feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or disconnected from yourself, this is for you.

What do I get from the call?

You'll receive a 30-minute 1:1 video call focused entirely on you, resulting in clarity on your next steps. This is followed by a personalized roadmap based on the iNAMS™ framework and a follow-up document with tools, resources, and actionable tips.

Is this therapy or medical advice?

No. This is a coaching and strategy session designed to provide clarity, support, and a roadmap for your personal wellness journey. It is not a substitute for professional therapy or medical advice from a qualified provider.

What happens after I book?

After your payment is processed, you'll be directed to my calendar to book your session. You'll also receive a confirmation email with a link to a private intake form. Please fill this out at least 24 hours before our session so I can come prepared.

What if I need to reschedule?

Life happens! You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment using the link in your confirmation email. Please note that I cannot accommodate rescheduling requests within the 24-hour window before our session.

What’s your refund policy?

Due to the energetic commitment and digital nature of this service, there are no refunds once a session is booked. Please ensure you are ready to commit to this step for yourself before making the purchase.

What is the true cost of waiting?

Living in burnout has a lethal expense, both morally and financially. Because we are dedicated to preserving life, we encourage you to see this not as a cost, but as an investment in yourself. Booking your call today means investing in your tomorrow with the proven, life-affirming systems that iNAMS™ offers.
